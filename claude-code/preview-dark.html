Dark Theme Principles

  • Near Black (#141413) replaces Parchment as the ambient background
  • Dark Surface (#30302e) replaces Ivory as the elevated container surface
  • Ivory (#faf9f5) replaces Near Black as the primary text color
  • Warm Silver (#b0aea5) serves as body text -- warm, never cool gray
  • Coral (#d97757) replaces Near Black for links and text accents
  • Terracotta (#c96442) remains unchanged -- the constant across both themes
